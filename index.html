Shadowlands Dungeons For Dummies

There are four difficulties consisting of Normal (50-60), Heroic (60), Mythic (60), and Mythic+ (60).

Knowing what loot drops in each dungeon can be very helpful in gearing your character, especially during the first few weeks of Shadowlands. We have prepared a dungeon loot guide for the Shadowlands dungeons that display not only what gear drops in each dungeon, but also Conduits and Legendary Memories!

Each different dungeon offers a new expierence to test your skills and progress deeper into the shadowlands L.O.R.E

The Necrotic Wake

The Necrotic Wake is a leveling dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Bastion. The Necrotic Wake has 4 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.

Plaguefall

Plaguefall is a leveling dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Maldraxxus. Plaguefall has 4 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.

Mists of Tirna Scithe

Mists of Tirna Scithe is a leveling dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Ardenweald. Mists of Tirna Scithe has 3 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.

Halls of Atonement

Halls of Atonement is a leveling dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Revendreth. Halls of Atonement has 4 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.

Theater of Pain

Theater of Pain is a max-level dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Maldraxxus. Theater of Pain has 5 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.

De Other Side

De Other Side is a max-level dungeon in Shadowlands, located in Ardenweald. De Other Side has 4 boss encounters. The dungeon is available in four difficulties: Normal, Heroic, Mythic, and Mythic+.
